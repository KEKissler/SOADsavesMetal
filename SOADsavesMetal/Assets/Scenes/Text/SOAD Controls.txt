SOAD Saves Metal Controls:

MOVEMENT:
Left and right arrow keys for movement
(Single) Down arrow key for crouch
(Double) Down arrow key to drop from platform
(Single) Spacebar or Up arrow key to jump
(Double) Spacebar or Up arrow key for unique aerial movement

ATTACKS:
Z key for short range attack
X key for long range attack (hold down for continuous flow)
C key for super attack (once meter is full)

ESC to pause

Note:
1. Characters with fixed super attacks gain immunity while attack is perfomed
2. Short range attacks can be used to extinguish projectiles 
3. Super meter gets a fill-boost when the boss is hit by a short range attack
