It all began one fateful night when the moon turned blood red and the ground split in twain…

Four Goddesses of the Underworld emerged to take back what was rightfully theirs—the very essence of Heavy Metal. One by one, bands lost their motivation to jam. 

It seemed no one could defeat these Mistresses of Evil, until System of a Down stepped in to reclaim the genre and gift it back to the Metalheads. They need your aid in ridding our realm of these horrifying monstrosities. Do you have what it takes to help SOAD Save Metal?
